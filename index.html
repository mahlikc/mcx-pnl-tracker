FAQ

Questions?

Do I need to connect my ad accounts?+
No. Metrik doesn't require any API connections or integrations. You manually log your daily numbers — spend, revenue, orders, new customers — which takes about 30 seconds per client. This gives you full control over your data and means zero setup friction.
What metrics does Metrik track?+
Over 50 metrics including ROAS, BEROAS, MER, aMER, CPA, nCPA, CAC, BECAC, net profit, gross profit margin, weighted GPM, new customer %, IAO profitability, commission, smart forecast, creative fatigue, scaling score, and more. Every metric is auto-calculated from your daily entries.
What are "collections"?+
Collections let you group your daily entries into named time periods — like "Q1 2026" or "Free Shipping Test" — each with their own margin and AOV settings. You can compare performance across collections to see how offer or margin changes affected results, with full scaling signals per collection.
How does the scaling engine work?+
The engine scores each client on 9 weighted signals: ROAS vs breakeven, net margin, aMER, MER gap (attribution check), CPA vs breakeven CPA, CAC efficiency, new customer ratio, creative fatigue, and budget cooldown. It then produces a verdict — SCALE HARD, SCALE, NUDGE UP, HOLD, TRIM, CUT, or PAUSE — with the exact reasoning and recommended budget percentage change.
Can my clients see my other clients?+
Never. Each client portal is completely isolated. Clients log in with a unique access code and can only see their own metrics, daily log, tips, and offers. Your portfolio-level data is never exposed.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel before your 14-day trial ends and you won't be charged at all. After that, cancel anytime from your billing settings — your access continues until the end of your billing period.
Is my data secure?+
Your data is stored securely with Supabase (built on PostgreSQL with row-level security). Authentication is handled through secure email/password login, and all data is isolated per agency account. Client portal access codes are separate from your main login.
